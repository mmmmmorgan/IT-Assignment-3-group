Brian

After an initial hurdle in gathering and communicating with group members, the following weeks went smoothly. Due to the usage of scheduling software and MS Teams communication, group members were able to provide their availabilities for a group meeting each week. The allocation of group work also went well, with each member contributing fairly and efficiently, and allowed for members to focus their efforts to produce higher quality content within a given timeframe. Project meeting minutes were met each week, with each meeting having clear agendas and discussions on the project’s tasks. However, improvements could be made, such as difficulties in scheduling due to uncontrollable circumstances. What surprised me was the discussion and contribution of each member during the meetings online, and especially during face-to-face meetings. I learned that each member could contribute to a project in their own way to maximize quality and different perspectives.
